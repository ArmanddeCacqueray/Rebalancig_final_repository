=================================
GLOSSARY
=================================

station
    Unique station identifier.

binary_index
    Hierarchical spatial index for stations (KD-tree encoding).
    Used to perform fast spatial selections, clustering, or to train
    models on small spatial groups of stations (typically blocks of
    about 64 nearby stations).

spatial_feature
    Static geographical or infrastructure features associated with each station.

ferie
    French term for public holidays.

component
    Latent components obtained from Singular Value Decomposition:
    Stock ≈ U @ S @ V^T

flux_plus
    Bike arrival flow (number of bikes returned to the station per hour).

flux_minus
    Bike departure flow (number of bikes taken from the station per hour).

vae
    Electric bikes ("Vélo à Assistance Électrique").

vm
    Mechanical bikes ("Vélo Mécanique").

vae_transporte, vm_transporte
    Rebalancing operations performed by the Velib operator
    (Smovengo). These represent bikes transported by trucks
    between stations.

operative
    Boolean flag indicating whether the station is operational.

meteo_dim
    Hourly meteorological variables provided by the weather service.

=================================
MAIN VARIABLES
=================================

demand /!\ Experimental, should be taken as a rough estimation
    Estimated latent demand (free of rebalancing operation and free of censorship due to saturation).

reg
    Regulation flag (rebalancing operation in progress)

cap
    Effective station capacity (migth variate over time and thus be different of "capacite")
vm_disp
    Available mechanical bikes.

vae_disp
    Available electric bikes.

indisp
    Number of unavailable bikes (maintenance, battery, etc.).

docks
    Number of available docking points.
